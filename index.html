About

I am Nicolò Paracchini, a curious and multifaceted personality with a deep passion for both artistic and technological realms.

My career spans across various fields, allowing me to develop a versatile and multidisciplinary approach.


As a Sound Engineer, I have worked in multiple studios, venues, and festivals, collaborating with over 2000 different artists, including names such as Umberto Maria Giardini, Mondo Marcio, Vacca, Mattak, Caneda, Bassi Maestro, Young Signorino and many others.


Although my primary role is that of an audio engineer, I identify foremost as a Composer and Musician, with my musical range extending from classical to jazz and even metal.


I have deeply studied classical and jazz harmony, obtaining a bachelor in Writing and Production from CPM Music Institute in Milan.

Honestly, what I enjoy the most is immersing myself in the process of creating music.


I have been playing bass and guitar for many years, touring across Europe, having worked with professional bands and musicians from around the globe on projects spanning a variety of genres.


In addition, I have created and implemented audio for various video games through my collaboration with KR Games, where I was entrusted with the entire audio department of their projects, handling everything from sound design to soundtrack production.

I've also cover video editing and video production aspect for trailers and gameplays.

Currently, I am furthering my expertise with FMOD and the software Unreal Engine to better integrate audio in future projects.


On the technological side, I am an experienced Android Developer, skilled in creating and optimizing mobile applications that deliver smooth and intuitive user experiences. In addition, as a Full Stack Web Developer, I work with various front-end technologies to build dynamic and engaging web applications.

My primary focus as a Tester allows me to ensure the highest standards of quality and performance in software, utilizing comprehensive testing methodologies to enhance reliability and user satisfaction.


I have a YouTube channel with over a thousand subscribers, where I explore advanced harmony applied to metal music.


My curiosity also led me to explore deep and intriguing topics such as philosophy and ancient civilizations.


This fusion of experiences and passions has provided me with a comprehensive understanding of the intersection between technology and art, allowing me to seamlessly merge the two worlds and craft my own distinctive style and aesthetic.
